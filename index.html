How do I order?

To order, simply add whichever products you like to your cart and checkout using your preferred payment method (Cash on delivery is available)

I dont have a PayPal account, how do I pay by card?

You can use PayPal without an account as a guest and pay using Credit or Debit card. Simply select the PayPal option at checkout, when redirected to PayPal, select the "Pay by Credit/Debit Card" option.

When will my products arrive?

If you have placed your order before 8pm, your order will be delivered on the following working day, for example, order before 8pm Sunday to receive products on Monday.

What days are you open?

We operate 3 days a week. Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays. We operate on certain national holidays, please check our announcements for updates.

Do you accept custom orders?

We do our best to accommodate any types of orders, please email baitlehembakery@gmail.com with any inquiries.
